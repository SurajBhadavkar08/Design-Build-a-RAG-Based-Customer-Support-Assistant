Customer Support FAQ Document for E-Commerce Store

1. Shipping Policy
We offer two shipping options:
- Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days, $5.99 or free on orders over $50
- Express Shipping: 1-2 business days, $12.99

Shipping is available to all 50 US states. Tracking information is provided within 24 hours of shipment.

2. Returns and Refunds
Our 30-day return policy allows returns on unused items in original packaging.
- Refunds processed within 7 business days to original payment method
- Return shipping is customer responsibility unless defective
- Certain items (e.g., personalized, hygiene) are non-returnable

To initiate a return, log into your account and use the Returns Portal.

3. Order Status and Tracking
- Order Confirmation: Email sent immediately after purchase
- Processing: 1-2 business days
- Shipped: Tracking email with carrier details
- Delivered: Confirm receipt in account dashboard

Use our Track Order page with your order ID.

4. Payment Methods and Issues
Accepted payments:
- Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- All transactions PCI-DSS compliant

If payment declined:
- Check card details and sufficient funds
- Try alternative payment method
- Contact your bank for fraud holds

5. Account Management
- Create account for faster checkout and order history
- Forgot password? Use recovery link
- Update shipping address, payment methods in Profile
- Download invoices from Order History

6. Product Availability and Backorders
- In-stock items ship immediately
- Backordered items: notified when available, no charge until shipped
- Inventory updated real-time

7. Common Issues
Q: My order hasn't arrived yet?
A: Check tracking; contact support if >7 days past estimate.

Q: Item damaged/missing?
A: Contact support within 7 days with photos.

Complex Queries (Escalate to Agent):
- Bulk/wholesale orders (>10 items)
- International shipping exceptions
- Fraud/dispute claims
- Custom product requests
- Legal/compliance questions

For all escalations, our system routes to live agent during business hours (9AM-6PM EST).
